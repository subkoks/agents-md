# Memecoin Trading Risk Playbook

## When to Use

- Building a meme-coin trading framework
- Reviewing discretionary or bot strategy risk
- Defining entry/exit and invalidation rules
- Managing drawdown and tail-risk exposure

## What This Skill Provides

- Position sizing model with max-loss caps
- Trade-quality checklist before entry
- Exit framework (profit-taking + invalidation)
- Post-trade review template for iteration

## Trade-Quality Checklist

- Liquidity depth supports intended size with acceptable slippage
- Holder concentration not deteriorating sharply
- Smart-money participation not in clear decline
- Netflow and volume confirm, not diverge
- No immediate event/lock/authority uncertainty

## Hard Rules

- Predefine stop/invalidation before entering.
- Cap per-trade and per-day risk in percentage terms.
- Avoid averaging losers in illiquid regimes.
- Do not use one metric as sole signal.

## Done Criteria

- Strategy doc has explicit risk limits and kill-switch rules.
- Every trade journal includes thesis, invalidation, and result.
- Weekly review tracks hit-rate, payoff ratio, and max drawdown.
